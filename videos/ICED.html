The Incredible, Changeable E-D

Video to teach kids about pronouncing past tense verbs.

So why do Americans pronounce "ed" three different ways? In the tradition of The Electric Company and Sesame St., I decided to create this video for kids describing how to use the past tense suffix "ed".
