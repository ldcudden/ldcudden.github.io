Sequoia Pathfinder Academy at Eastmark Commercial

A commercial for Sequoia Pathfinder Academy at Eastmark concentrating on their STEAM curriculum principles - Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Math. This was part of a marketing campaign which included logo design, brochure, door hanger and t-shirt design which was also provided by Lynne Cudden Video and Design.

Producing, directing, lighting, camera, music selection, graphics, motion graphics, editing, color correction, audio recording, and audio balance, STEAM logo design. Used Adobe Creative Suite.
